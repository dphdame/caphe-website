## Voice Quick Reference

- **Tone:** Professional-accessible
- **Formality:** More formal than tooearlytosay, avoids stiff bureaucratic language
- **Pronouns:** First-person plural ("we," "our")
- **Signature phrases:** "Practical training in health economics." / "For California public health practitioners."
- **Avoid:** Overly casual language, second person, jargon without context, marketing hype
