About Us

Building a community of economists committed to enhancing public health in California.

Who We Are

A learning collaborative of economists dedicated to applying data-driven methods (e.g., cost-effectiveness analysis and causal inference) to public health challenges in California.

Our Mission

  • Professional Development: We provide mentorship programs to nurture the next generation of public health economists.
  • Collaborative Research: We facilitate joint projects that offer analytical and applied insights to improve health policies and programs.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: We work to increase recognition of health economics' impact on delivering meaningful, high-impact services.

What We Do

  • Hold monthly economist-only sessions for peer review and skill-building.
  • Host quarterly open webinars for broader engagement.
  • Form working groups to create shared resources (white papers, code repositories) and deepen analytical expertise.

Our Approach

  • Focus on data-driven solutions that distinguish economics from standard epidemiological practices.
  • Emphasize open knowledge-sharing through voluntary leadership and peer-based collaboration.
  • Strive to maintain a supportive community for both new and experienced health economists.

Get Involved

Interested in joining our community? We welcome economists working in public health departments, academic institutions, and consulting organizations across California.
