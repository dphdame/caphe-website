Interested in Membership?

CAPHE membership is open to economists working in public health across California. Benefits include:

  • Access to monthly peer review sessions
  • Discounts on paid workshops
  • Working group participation
  • Webinar recording archive
  • Member directory access

Select "Membership Inquiry" in the form to learn more about joining.
