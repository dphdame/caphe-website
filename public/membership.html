Join Us

Join the CAPHE Community

There are two ways to engage with CAPHE: as a Member (for trained economists) or as an Affiliate (for everyone interested in public health economics).

For Economists

Become a Member

Full membership is for trained economists working in public health. Members get access to peer review sessions, working groups, and the webinar archive.

Eligibility: PhD in economics (or related field), Master's with econometrics training, or current PhD student. See details ↓

Member Benefits:

  • Peer Review Sessions (present your work, get feedback)
  • Working Groups (collaborate on shared resources)
  • Webinar Archive (recordings of past presentations)
  • Public webinars and resource announcements

For Everyone

Become an Affiliate

Affiliates are public health professionals, researchers, policymakers, and others interested in health economics. Stay connected to CAPHE's public work and events.

No eligibility requirements. Just provide your email to receive updates.

Affiliate Benefits:

  • Public webinar invitations
  • White paper and resource announcements
  • Tool release notifications
  • Event updates (1-2 emails/month)

Who Can Join as a Member

Membership Eligibility

CAPHE membership is specifically designed for trained economists working in public health. This focus ensures our peer review sessions, working groups, and discussions operate at the appropriate technical level.

To be eligible, you must meet at least one of the following:

  • 1. PhD in Economics or a related quantitative field (health economics, agricultural economics, public policy with economics focus)
  • 2. Master's degree with formal training in econometrics, causal inference, or economic evaluation methods
  • 3. Current enrollment in a PhD program in economics or a related field

Don't meet these criteria? You can still stay connected by joining as an Affiliate to receive updates on public events and new resources.
