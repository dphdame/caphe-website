Professional Membership Application

Join California's community of public health economists.

CAPHE professional membership connects economists working to advance public health research, policy, and practice across California. We welcome applications from those with doctoral or master's-level training in economics or econometrics. Applications are typically reviewed within 5 business days.

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for Professional Membership, you must meet at least one of the following:

  • PhD in Economics (including health economics, agricultural/applied economics)
  • Master's degree in Economics with training in econometrics or causal inference
  • Current enrollment in a PhD program in economics

Don't meet these criteria? Join as a Community Member instead.

Membership Benefits

  • Peer Review Sessions (present your work, get feedback)
  • Working Groups (collaborate on shared resources)
  • Member-only webinar sessions and learning labs
  • Webinar archive and all Community Member benefits
