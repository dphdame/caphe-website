# Antigravity Prompt: Comparing Two Programs - Cost-Effectiveness Visual

## Primary Visual: Cost-Effectiveness Comparison Diagram

### Prompt

Clean, professional infographic comparing two health programs on white background.

**Layout:** Horizontal two-panel comparison with center decision element.

**Left Panel - Program A (Teal):**
- Rounded rectangle with teal (`#0D9488`) border and light teal fill
- Header: "Program A" in bold
- Icon: Simple person-group icon representing community health workers
- Three metric rows:
  - "Cost: $450,000"
  - "QALYs: 48"
  - "$/QALY: $9,375"

**Right Panel - Program B (Gold):**
- Rounded rectangle with gold (`#F9A825`) border and light gold fill
- Header: "Program B" in bold
- Icon: Simple smartphone/app icon representing digital platform
- Three metric rows:
  - "Cost: $320,000"
  - "QALYs: 60"
  - "$/QALY: $5,333"

**Center Element:**
- Large "VS" in primary blue (`#0041A5`)
- Below: Arrow pointing to Program B with text "More health per dollar"

**Bottom Banner:**
- Light gray background strip
- Text: "Both programs work. The question is which delivers more value."

Style: Flat design, no gradients, no shadows. Minimalist infographic style.
Montserrat font, semi-bold weight. Clean geometric icons.
Rounded corners (8px) on all elements.

## Secondary Visual: ICER Concept Diagram (Optional)

### Prompt

Educational diagram explaining Incremental Cost-Effectiveness Ratio (ICER) on white background.

**Layout:** Cost-effectiveness plane with annotations.

**Axes:**
- X-axis: "Health Gain (QALYs)" with arrow pointing right
- Y-axis: "Cost ($)" with arrow pointing up
- Origin labeled "Current Practice"

**Quadrants labeled:**
- Upper left: "More costly, less effective" (red tint, label: "Dominated")
- Lower right: "Less costly, more effective" (green tint, label: "Dominant")
- Upper right: "More costly, more effective" (yellow tint, label: "Evaluate ICER")
- Lower left: "Less costly, less effective" (yellow tint, label: "Evaluate ICER")

**Key Elements:**
- Diagonal dashed line from origin through upper right representing "$50,000/QALY threshold"
- Two dots: Program A (teal) and Program B (gold) positioned in the plane
- Formula box in corner: "ICER = (Cost_B - Cost_A) / (QALY_B - QALY_A)"

Style: Clean academic diagram style. Primary blue for axes and labels.
Light quadrant shading. Professional but accessible.
