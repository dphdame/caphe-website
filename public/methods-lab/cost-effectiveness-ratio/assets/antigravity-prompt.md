# Antigravity Prompt: Cost-Effectiveness Ratio Conceptual Diagram

## Prompt

Clean, professional conceptual diagram on white background showing the ICER decision framework.

**Layout:** Horizontal flow from left to right with 3 main sections.

**Section 1 - Inputs (Left side):**
Two stacked rounded rectangle nodes:
- Top node: Teal (`#0D9488`) background, white text "Incremental Cost"
- Bottom node: Gold (`#F9A825`) background, white text "Incremental Effect"

**Section 2 - Calculation (Center):**
Large rounded rectangle node with Primary Blue (`#0041A5`) border (3px), white fill:
- Large text: "ICER"
- Smaller text below: "Cost per QALY"
- Formula shown: fraction with "Delta Cost" over "Delta Effect"

**Section 3 - Decision (Right side):**
Diamond decision node with Primary Blue (`#0041A5`) fill, white text:
- Text: "ICER < WTP?"

Below the diamond, two outcome boxes:
- Left outcome: Green (`#2E7D32`) rounded rectangle, white text "Adopt"
- Right outcome: Orange (`#F57C00`) rounded rectangle, white text "Reject"

**Arrows:**
- Teal arrow from "Incremental Cost" to ICER box (top entry)
- Gold arrow from "Incremental Effect" to ICER box (bottom entry)
- Gray (`#424242`) arrow from ICER box to decision diamond
- Green dashed arrow from diamond down-left to "Adopt" (labeled "Yes")
- Orange dashed arrow from diamond down-right to "Reject" (labeled "No")

**Additional element:**
Small text label above the decision diamond: "Willingness-to-Pay Threshold" in gray text (`#757575`)

Style: Flat design, no gradients except subtle on main boxes, no heavy shadows. Minimalist infographic style.
Montserrat font, semi-bold weight for labels, regular for smaller text.
Arrows have small triangular arrowheads.
Nodes have 8px rounded corners and generous padding.

The visual story: Costs and effects feed into the ICER calculation, which is then compared against a threshold to determine whether to adopt or reject the intervention.

- **Teal (#0D9488):** Represents costs/resources
- **Gold (#F9A825):** Represents health effects/outcomes

- **Green (#2E7D32):** Positive decision (adopt)
- **Orange (#F57C00):** Negative decision (reject/wait)
