# Antigravity Prompt: Measuring Health in a Common Unit - Conceptual Diagram

## Prompt

Clean, professional conceptual diagram on white background illustrating how QALYs convert different health outcomes to a common unit.

Left side shows three separate boxes representing different outcome types:
- Top box (Teal `#0D9488`): "Deaths Prevented" with "12 lives" text
- Middle box (Gold `#F9A825`): "Quality Improvement" with "400 people" text
- Bottom box (Blue `#0041A5`): "Disability Reduced" with "200 people" text

Center shows a conversion funnel or process indicator with text "QALY Conversion" in gray (`#424242`)

Right side shows three horizontal bars of different lengths, all in the same color (Teal `#0D9488`), representing the converted QALY values:
- Top bar (shortest): "108 QALYs"
- Middle bar (longest): "600 QALYs"
- Bottom bar (medium): "250 QALYs"

Arrows flow from each left box through the center conversion area to the corresponding right bar.

Below the diagram, a caption in gray italic text: "Different outcome types, converted to a common unit"

Style: Flat design, no gradients, minimal shadows. Clean infographic style.
Montserrat font, semi-bold weight for labels, regular for numbers.
Boxes have 8px rounded corners.
