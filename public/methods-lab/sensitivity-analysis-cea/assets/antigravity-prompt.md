# Antigravity Prompt: Sensitivity Analysis Conceptual Diagram

## Prompt

Clean, professional infographic diagram on white background showing the concept of sensitivity analysis in cost-effectiveness analysis.

Three sections arranged horizontally:

**Left Section - "Point Estimate":**
- Single blue circle (Primary Blue #0041A5) with "ICER: $65K" text
- Arrow pointing to green checkmark below
- Label: "Base Case Decision"

**Middle Section - "One-Way Sensitivity":**
- Horizontal bar extending left and right from center point
- Left side teal (#0D9488), right side gold (#F9A825)
- Center point aligned with left section's ICER
- Vertical dashed line at $100K threshold
- Label: "Parameter Range"

**Right Section - "Probabilistic (PSA)":**
- Scatter cloud of 30+ small dots
- Mix of green (#2E7D32) and orange (#F57C00) dots
- Dashed diagonal line representing WTP threshold
- Label: "1,000 Simulations"

Bottom text in dark gray (#424242):
"From certainty to probability: How confident should we be?"

Style: Flat design, no gradients except subtle shading. Minimalist infographic style.
Montserrat font, semi-bold weight for labels, regular for supporting text.
Clean lines, generous whitespace between sections.

## Alternative Prompt: Decision Uncertainty Flow

Clean, professional flow diagram showing how uncertainty propagates through a cost-effectiveness decision.

Four connected nodes arranged in a horizontal flow:

**Node 1 - "Input Parameters"** (Teal #0D9488):
- Rounded rectangle
- Icon: Stack of variables (cost, effect, utility)
- Small text: "Each has uncertainty"

**Arrow 1:** Solid gray (#424242) with label "Distributions"

**Node 2 - "Model"** (Primary Blue #0041A5):
- Rounded rectangle
- Icon: Calculator/equation symbol
- Small text: "ICER = Delta C / Delta E"

**Arrow 2:** Solid gray with label "Monte Carlo"

**Node 3 - "Results Cloud"** (Split - Green #2E7D32 / Orange #F57C00):
- Oval/cloud shape containing scattered dots
- Mix of green and orange dots
- Small text: "1,000+ scenarios"

**Arrow 3:** Solid gray with label "Summarize"

**Node 4 - "Decision Confidence"** (Accent Gold #F9A825):
- Rounded rectangle
- Large text: "78%"
- Small text: "Probability cost-effective"

Style: Flat design, no shadows. Flow diagram style with clear directional arrows.
Montserrat font. Nodes have 8px rounded corners and generous padding.
