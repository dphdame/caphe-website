# Antigravity Prompt: Measurement and Instrumentation Threat DAG

## Prompt

Clean, professional directed acyclic graph (DAG) diagram on white background.
4 rounded rectangle nodes arranged in a diamond formation:

**Top center:** "Measurement System Change" - Primary Blue (#0041A5) background, white text
**Middle left:** "Observed Treatment (Wellness Program)" - Teal (#0D9488) background, white text
**Middle right:** "Observed Outcome (Visit Counts)" - Gold (#F9A825) background, dark text
**Bottom center:** "True Care Delivery (unchanged)" - Light gray (#E0E0E0) background, dark text

Arrows:
1. From "Measurement System Change" to "Observed Outcome" - solid dark gray (#424242) arrow
2. From "Measurement System Change" to "Observed Treatment" - solid dark gray arrow (curved slightly)
3. From "Observed Treatment" to "Observed Outcome" - dashed green (#2E7D32) arrow with "?" label
4. From "True Care Delivery" to "Observed Outcome" - dotted gray (#757575) arrow (subtle)

Style: Flat design, no gradients, no shadows. Minimalist infographic style.
Montserrat font, semi-bold weight. Arrows have small arrowheads.
Nodes have 8px rounded corners and generous padding.

Key visual emphasis: The "Measurement System Change" node should be prominent at the top, with arrows flowing down to show how it affects what we observe, while "True Care Delivery" at the bottom shows the reality we cannot directly see after the measurement change.
