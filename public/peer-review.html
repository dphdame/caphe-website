Present your work-in-progress and receive constructive feedback from fellow health economists.

How It Works

  1. Request a slot

    Submit your request at least one week before the meeting

  2. Confirmation

    You'll receive an email confirming your slot

  3. Prepare materials

    Share slides or documents 48 hours before the meeting

  4. Present & discuss

    15-30 minutes of presentation and Q&A
