Working Groups

Member-led groups that pursue research questions of shared interest and receive feedback from the larger CAPHE community. Outputs are shared with the CAPHE community and public.

  • ✓ Cost-Effectiveness Methods — Standardized approaches and R/Python packages
  • ✓ Causal Inference Applications — Policy evaluation with replicable code
  • ✓ Methods Lab — Interactive modules with California case studies

How It Works

Groups meet monthly to discuss progress, troubleshoot methods, and share resources. Members propose new groups based on shared research interests.

Format: Monthly working sessions + async collaboration
Output: White papers, code libraries, and analytical tools

Peer Review Sessions

Present your work-in-progress to fellow economists and get constructive feedback. A supportive environment to refine your analysis before formal publication or presentation to leadership.

  • ✓ Request 15, 30, or 45 minutes for your presentation
  • ✓ Confidential, supportive environment
  • ✓ Last Wednesday of each month, 12:00 - 1:00 PM PT

How It Works

Format: 15-45 min presentation + discussion
