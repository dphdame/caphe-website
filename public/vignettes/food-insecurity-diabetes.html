The Big Question

These two things clearly go together. But does lack of food access actually cause more hospitalizations? Or does poverty cause both problems at the same time?

Conduct a Cluster RCT or Staggered Rollout Pilot

Rationale

Given the ambiguity in the causal evidence, we recommend a rigorous pilot study before scaling interventions. This approach allows for:

  • Causal identification through randomization or quasi-experimental design
  • Cost-effectiveness estimation with credible counterfactuals
  • Learning about implementation challenges before full rollout

Proposed Design

Implement a staggered rollout of food prescription programs across willing counties, with careful documentation of timing to enable difference-in-differences analysis.

Data Requirements

  • Pre-intervention baseline on diabetes hospitalizations (minimum 2 years)
  • Food prescription program enrollment and utilization data
  • Individual-level linkage to hospital discharge data where possible
  • County-level controls for concurrent policy changes
